NASA Glenn Research Center – 120-Hour Engineering Shadowing Experience

Jun 2022 - Aug 2022

During a 120-hour shadowing program at NASA Glenn Research Center, I gained firsthand exposure to two key divisions. In Space Communications and Spectrum Management, I attended project meetings for the Communications Services Project. In the Engine Research Division, I assisted with ASCR Gas Bench calibration, observed supersonic wind tunnel experiments, and supported test preparation efforts. This experience deepened my understanding of NASA’s interdisciplinary engineering work and real-world testing environments.
